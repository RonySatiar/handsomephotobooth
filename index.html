Welcome to Handsome Photobooth

We provide photo booth rental to the Bay Area, and specialize in all events!

Looking to add that extra touch of fun and excitement to your upcoming event? Look no further!
We are Handsome Photobooth, the premier family-owned photo booth business in the Bay Area. Our state-of-the-art photo booth is here to capture candid moments and create unforgettable memories that will keep your guests talking for months to come.


Our photos are taken with a DSLR camera, instantly downloadable via QR code. Enjoy unlimited high-quality 2x6 or 4x6 prints to take home and cherish. Plus, our personalized photobooth templates, designed by our talented designer, ensure a unique and customized experience that matches your event's theme perfectly.


We go the extra mile to provide a modern sequin backdrop, sanitized and entertaining props that will unleash your guests' inner comedians, and a complimentary photo album to preserve all those hilarious. And don't worry about sharing the memories - we offer a downloadable album on Google Photos for easy sharing and laughter. Our professional booth attendant will be there to assist your guests, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone. With professional studio-grade lighting, we guarantee picture-perfect moments.


Don't settle for a bland event. Make it extraordinary with Handsome Photobooth!
Contact us today to book our photo booth for your next event and let's make some memories!
